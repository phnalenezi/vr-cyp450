# Storyboard — Acetaminophen First Build

## Title
Immersive Visualization of Cytochrome P450–Mediated Drug Metabolism  
Exploring Binding, Oxidation, and Molecular Transformation Inside the Liver

## Learning goal
The user enters a liver-cell VR scene and observes a simplified, educational visualization of the CYP-mediated oxidative branch of acetaminophen metabolism, ending with the appearance of the reactive metabolite NAPQI.

## User experience flow

### Step 1 — Enter the scene
The user appears inside a cell-like environment and sees the CYP450 region ahead.

### Step 2 — Orientation
A text panel explains that this lesson isolates one metabolic pathway for teaching purposes.

### Step 3 — Drug approach
Acetaminophen is shown moving toward the active site.

### Step 4 — Binding pose
The drug is positioned closer to the heme region.

### Step 5 — Oxidative branch
The panel explains that the CYP-mediated oxidative branch can generate NAPQI.

### Step 6 — Metabolite display
The model changes from acetaminophen to NAPQI.

### Step 7 — Educational takeaway
The user learns why NAPQI matters clinically and chemically.
